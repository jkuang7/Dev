Aspida is a tech-driven insurance carrier focused on annuity and life insurance products.
The Lead Engineer role supports design, development, and maintenance of system software,
including CRM, CCM, vendor products, and cloud solutions. The role collaborates with
business users, product owners, developers, testers, and deployment teams; leads technical
delivery across SDLC; troubleshoots production issues; and ensures compliance with
enterprise architecture/governance standards. Preferred tools include Zendesk, SmartCom,
and Axway. Core stack includes Node.js, React, AWS, and DynamoDB.
